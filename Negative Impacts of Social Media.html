Unconcious Negative Impacts of Social Media

  • People can use filters & photoshop to make themselves look better on social media
    • “Growing up in the age of social media can give the children and young adults on these
      apps insecurity over their features in photos. Users feel the need to correct themselves
      to be acceptable for social media and are compelled to utilize an editing app before posting
      on social media.” - Erika Ryan, Senior at Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School
    • Not expressing who they are → Posting what they want to be
    • Social Health involves having positive rewarding friendships with others
    • Teens tend to make friends with ppl. across social media
    • These friends that the teens have tend to appreciate the teens for who they appear to be on social media, but not their true self
    • Teens aren’t having rewarding friendships → Social health negatively affected
    • Physical, mental, & social health linked → If one of these health components affected, all will be affected eventually
